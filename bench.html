  • SPRINGTIME BLOOMS

    When taking this image, I considered how the natural environment interacts with manmade objects that show how society has progressed and industrialized. In this image, I think about the ways that the flowers bloom and extend through the gaps in the metal bench. To me, this shows how nature always has a way of building itself around us.
