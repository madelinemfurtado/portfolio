DEBORAH BUTTERFIELD

I find a lot of inspiration from works of art. I have a specific interest in modern and contemporary art. I hope to use my design skills to develop future exhibitions that I may work on in my career. I had the ability to intern at the UC Davis Jan Shrem and Maria Manetti Shrem Museum in 2023, and that experience allowed me to meet several contemporary artists including Deborah Butterfield. This image is a picture of one of her bronze horses. She takes pieces of wood and arranges them to make sculptures that are an abstraction of the figure of horses. She uses a technique similar to lost-wax bronze casting, where she burns out the wood from the mold and then pours in bronze. She then treats the bronze sculpture with heat and chemicals as a way to "paint," which creates the outward appearance of wood, even though the sculpture is bronze..
