BAROQUE PEARL OF THE SEA

For this jewelry design, I was inspired by the period of the baroque in Europe. This design uses 14k gold-filled wire, gold chain, and freshwater pearls. This design was the first necklace that I commissioned for someone. The client was pleased with the result, and this opportunity gave me a lot of experience in learning my craft and pitching myself as a small business. I wanted to emphasize the baroque concept of the "irregular pearl," which is why the pearls that dangle off the main chain are somewhat irregular in shape. The gold tone of the chain and wire elevates the look by making the pearls stand out. It reminds me of a jewelry piece you would find in a shipwreck, lost at sea and never to be found again.
