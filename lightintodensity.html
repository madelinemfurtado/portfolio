    "LIGHT INTO DENSITY: ABSTRACT ENCOUNTERS FROM 1920S-1960S" EXHIBITION AT THE MANETTI SHREM MUSEUM OF ART

    January - September 2024

    I was a Guest Exhibition Designer for the Manetti Shrem Museum of Art, where I worked in a team to create an exhibition design that focused on promoting visitor interpretations of the artworks on display. During my time in this position, I drafted exhibition text mockups for the interior spaces, designed title wall mockups, and assisted with spatial planning. Our main goal with our designs were to create a visitor-centered experience within the gallery space. The exhibition was on view at the Manetti Shrem Museum from September 2024 to May 2025.

    I worked on this project in a team of 6 other UC Davis Design students, with UCD Design Professors Tim McNeil and Brett Snyder as our supervisors. You can find more information on this project through the website "Storied Spaces" which is dedicated towards projects undertaken at UC Davis surrounding experiential design.
